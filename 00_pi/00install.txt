Configuration (2022)
-------------

*First Boot
   *Set Country
   *Setup user/password
   *Setup WiFi
*Second Boot
   *sh 00install.sh
   *Setup a screensaver
   *Configure audio if necessary
   *Run Calibration to verify LEDs
   *Optional - Append panel_config.txt to /boot for small panel (800x480)
   *Reboot
